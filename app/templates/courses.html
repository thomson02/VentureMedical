HSE Approved Workplace for first aid at work

Emergency First Aid at Work (1 Day)

HSE Approved one day Emergency First Aid at Work course is designed for smaller workplaces that present few health and safety risks to employees.

First Aid at Work (3 Day)

This course is designed for the larger employer where the staff numbers exceed 50 employees or for workplaces with more than five staff where the risks of injury are higher such as construction, engineering or manufacturing industries.

First Aid at Work Refresher (2 Day)

A course for the re-qualification of First Aiders that currently hold a valid First Aid at Work qualification but is due for renewal.

This 4 hours course includes basic life support and is designed for people who will have access to an Automated External Defibrillator.
