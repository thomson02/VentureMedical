This 2 hour course is designed to give information on Anaphylaxis and Anaphylactic Shock. It is a very popular course and is used by many Schools, Colleges, Universities, Early Years Teams, Child Minders, Nurseries and Parents as well as Youth Groups. It provides training in the use of an Epi-Pen / Ana Pen and knowledge and confidence to deal with this potentially and ever more common life threatening emergency.

On successful completion of the course the candidate is awarded with a Venture Medical certificate valid for three years. The certificate will state that the holder has received training in the safe use of an Epi Pen & Ana Pen.

The course syllabus includes:

  • Incident management and developing a care plan
  • Recognition of signs and symptoms
  • Management of an unconscious Child or Baby
  • Practical session using Epi Pen & Ana Pen training devices
