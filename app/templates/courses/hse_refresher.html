A Health & Safety Executive approved course that complies with the Health & Safety (First Aid) Regulations 1981 for the re-qualification of First Aiders that currently hold a valid First Aid at Work qualification but is due for renewal.

Please Note: You MUST have a valid FAW certificate prior to attending this course.

The course offers an overview and refresher of all subjects taught on the full 3 day First Aid at Work course. Any updates regarding techniques or legislation will also be imparted to the candidate.

Subjects include:

  • Aims and principles of First Aid
  • Basic life support
  • Management of the unconscious casualty
  • Assessment and treatment of a casualty who is wounded, bleeding or shocked
  • Assessment and treatment of fractures, dislocations and soft tissue injuries
  • Assessment and treatment of burns, poisoning and eye injuries
  • Assessment and treatment of common major and minor illnesses
  • Prevention of cross-infection during First Aid procedures
  • Emergency transport of casualties
  • Legal aspects of First Aid at Work - record keeping and use of equipment
