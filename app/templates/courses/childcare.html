This 12 hour course, which can be held over two consecutive days, four evenings or split over two separate weekends, is a requirement of all Childcare Practitioners who require registration by OFSTED. This includes Childminders, Nannies, Nursery Practitioners, Pre School and After School Club staff and anyone who will be working or caring for children.

It is also suitable for those who wish to work part time as baby sitters with the course having a minimum age requirement of 16 years of age to attend.

The course syllabus includes:

  • The role of the first aider
  • First aid priorities
  • Incident management
  • Basic Life Support
  • CPR for infants and children
  • Anaphylaxis and the use of Epi-pens
  • Bleeding, burns and scalds
  • Airway obstruction
  • Fractures, head, neck and back injuries
  • Poisoning
  • Bites and stings
  • Effects of extreme heat and cold
  • Febrile convulsions
  • Epilepsy
  • Asthma
  • Sickle cell anaemia
  • Diabetes
  • Meningitis
