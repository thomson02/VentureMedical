Parent and Family First Aid

A no nonsense one day first aid course for parents and families who want to know basic child and baby specific first aid. The course is designed around the needs of parents and families looking for a sound knowledge of first aid skills for the younger patient.

It is not suitable for professionals working in the field of child care who should attend the two day Paediatric First Aid course which fulfils the industry requirements.

The course syllabus includes:

  • CPR
  • Choking
  • Bleeding
  • Shock
  • Anaphylaxis
  • Poisoning
  • Febrile Convulsions
  • Meningitis
  • Fractures
  • Eye Injuries
  • Head Injuries
  • Burns and Scalds
  • Diabetes, Epilepsy, Asthma
  • Bites and Stings
