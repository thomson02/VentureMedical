This course is designed in conjunction with the clinical guidance documents from the UK Resuscitation Council for resuscitation emergencies within General and Dental Practices and allows practice staff to complete their yearly resuscitation updates.

This three hour course is certificated and fulfils the CPD criteria for General and Dental practices. It covers:

  • CPR for Adult and Child
  • Choking
  • Anaphylaxis
  • Asthma
  • Cardiac Emergencies
  • Epileptic Seizures
  • Hypoglycaemia
  • Syncope
  • Specialist equipment use and O2 therapy including OP airways and bag valve mask resuscitation.
