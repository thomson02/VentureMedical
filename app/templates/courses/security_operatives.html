A course like no other currently available on the market this goes beyond the basics and limitations of standard Emergency First Aid courses and instead teaches you, the front line practitioner, to deal with the type of injuries and emergencies you are realistically likely to encounter and the skills and equipment suited to allow you to save lives.

Using the most up to date training and equipment, many developed direct from current front lines in Iraq and Afghanistan, this no nonsense course looks at the type of trauma injuries your patrons or fellow staff may fall victim to and trains you how to deal with them quickly, efficiently and effectively.

Stand that bit further above the competition with the Venture Medical Security Trauma Course.

Successful candidates will be awarded the Venture Medical Certificate in Security Operatives Trauma Management valid for two years.

Using instructors with over twenty years experience in front line trauma injuries associated with the specialist field of security work, this one day course is ideal practical training for all those involved as an operative in the security industry who regularly deal with members of the public in entertainment venues.

The course syllabus includes:

  • The role of the Trauma trained first aider
  • First aid priorities
  • Safe operating areas and scene safety
  • Operating in the indoor environment
  • Chain of survival
  • Dealing with unconscious casualties
  • Immediate life threatening injuries and major trauma
  • Assisting Ambulance services
  • Medical handovers – what the medics need to know
  • Moving and evacuation
  • Advanced airway management
  • Specialist medical equipment
  • Trauma First aid kits fit for purpose
Cost: £75 per participant which includes certificate (valid for 2 years).
