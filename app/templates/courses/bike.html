Motorcycle accidents and fatalities are currently the lowest on record with a 9% reduction in the last year. [Dept. of Transport Annual Report 2012]

Good news, but accidents do still happen on an all too frequent basis and the immediate actions of others on scene can have an incredible effect on a positive outcome for casualties.

Our Bike Safe course is designed to help you assist with that positive outcome with training to provide immediate life-saving assistance until definitive medical care arrives. That’s what can make all the difference, the difference between life and death.

“Just do nothing” isn’t an option so we’ll teach you what to do, when to do it and how to do it right.

This three hour course is most definitely First Aid without the fuss and will cover:

  • Approaching and protecting the scene and the casualty
  • Mechanism of injury – dealing with Trauma
  • Spinal Immobilisation
  • Safe helmet removal – How to do it, when to do it and when to leave it on
  • Basic life support and CPR
